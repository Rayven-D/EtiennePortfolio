FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT APP (MOBILE) Designing a budgeting experience that prioritizes clarity, confidence, and approachable financial guidance.
PROJECT_OVERVIEW Designed a budgeting-focused financial app aimed at helping students better understand and manage their spending.

The core goal was to make budgeting feel achievable rather than overwhelming by presenting financial data in a clear, supportive, and easy-to-digest way. Focused on simplifying complex information through visual cues, intuitive structure, and interaction patterns that encourage confidence and ongoing engagement.
TARGET_AUDIENCE Designed for students managing personal finances for the first time.
  • Students learning to budget and track spending
  • Users who want simple, visual financial feedback
  • Individuals who may feel overwhelmed by traditional banking apps
  • Users looking for quick insights into spending habits
01
PHASE_01: RESEARCH [RESEARCH & DISCOVERY]
Explored how users, particularly students, interact with budgeting tools and identified key challenges around clarity, motivation, and financial anxiety.
  • Competitive analysis of budgeting and fintech apps
  • Research on student financial behaviors and needs
  • Identification of pain points in traditional budgeting tools
  • Analysis of how financial data is presented visually
  • Exploration of emotional response to financial tracking
02
PHASE_02: VISUAL DIRECTION [BRANDING & CONCEPT EXPLORATION]
Explored the visual direction of the app through sketching and branding development, focusing on creating an approachable and supportive experience.
  • Sketching layout concepts and early ideas
  • Development of branding (color, typography, tone)
  • Exploration of a friendly and non-intimidating visual language
03
PHASE_03: SYSTEM DEVELOPMENT [UI DESIGN, PROTOTYPING & DESIGN SYSTEM]
Developed the visual system and interface by translating concepts into high-fidelity designs and interactive prototypes. Focused on building a consistent design system that supports clarity, scalability, and ease of use.
  • Designed high-fidelity UI for core screens
  • Built interactive prototypes for key flows
  • Defined reusable components (buttons, cards, progress indicators)
  • Established typography, spacing, and color system
  • Created consistent patterns for budgeting and data visualization
  • Applied the system across all screens for cohesion
04
PHASE_04: VALIDATION [TESTING & ITERATION]
Tested designs to evaluate usability, clarity, and emotional tone, ensuring the experience felt supportive rather than overwhelming.
  • Usability testing on understanding budget visuals
  • Evaluation of how quickly users interpret spending status
  • Identification of friction in navigation and layout
  • Iteration on hierarchy and interaction patterns
  • Refinement based on team and user feedback
