FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT APP (MOBILE) Designing a budgeting experience that prioritizes clarity, confidence, and approachable financial guidance.
PROJECT_OVERVIEW Designed a budgeting-focused financial app aimed at helping students better understand and manage their spending.

The core goal was to make budgeting feel achievable rather than overwhelming by presenting financial data in a clear, supportive, and easy-to-digest way. Focused on simplifying complex information through visual cues, intuitive structure, and interaction patterns that encourage confidence and ongoing engagement.
TARGET_AUDIENCE Designed for students managing personal finances for the first time.
  • Students learning to budget and track spending
  • Users who want simple, visual financial feedback
  • Individuals who may feel overwhelmed by traditional banking apps
  • Users looking for quick insights into spending habits
01
PHASE_01: RESEARCH [RESEARCH & DISCOVERY]
Explored how users, particularly students, interact with budgeting tools and identified key challenges around clarity, motivation, and financial anxiety.
  • Competitive analysis of budgeting and fintech apps
  • Research on student financial behaviors and needs
  • Identification of pain points in traditional budgeting tools
  • Analysis of how financial data is presented visually
  • Exploration of emotional response to financial tracking
