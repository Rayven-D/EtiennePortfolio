UX/UI DESIGN - 2025
STREAMING PLATFORM UX Designing a cross-platform streaming experience with a focus on intuitive navigation and interaction across TV and mobile.
PROJECT_OVERVIEW Designed a cross-platform streaming experience using Reddit's brand system for both TV and mobile.
Focused on creating intuitive navigation and clear interaction across different input types, with special attention to remote-based navigation on TV. The goal was to make content browsing and discovery simple, consistent, and easy to use across devices.
TARGET_AUDIENCE Designed for users who browse and watch content across TV and mobile.
  • Users who want simple, intuitive navigation
  • Viewers using remote controls (TV) and touch (mobile)
  • People familiar with content-heavy platforms like Reddit or streaming apps
01
PHASE_01: RESEARCH [RESEARCH & DISCOVERY]
Conducted research on Reddit's brand system and analyzed streaming platform patterns to understand navigation behaviors across TV and mobile environments. Identified key usability challenges specific to remote-based interaction.
  • Analysis of Reddit brand guidelines (color, typography, tone)
  • Competitive audit of streaming platforms (Netflix, Apple TV, etc.)
  • Navigation pattern analysis for TV interfaces
  • Identification of pain points in remote-based navigation
  • Review of mobile vs TV interaction differences
02
PHASE_02: EARLY DESIGN [IDEATION & WIREFRAMING]
Explored multiple layout directions focusing on navigation clarity, content hierarchy, and cross-platform consistency. Developed low-fidelity structures to test usability and interaction flow.
  • Sketching and layout exploration (multiple navigation structures)
  • Information architecture for content browsing
  • Low-fidelity wireframes for TV and mobile
  • Exploration of horizontal vs vertical navigation systems
  • Early validation of navigation flows
03
PHASE_03: INTERACTIVE COMPONENTS [INTERACTION & PROTOTYPING]
Built interactive components and defined behavior systems for both TV and mobile interfaces. Focused on creating clear feedback and focus states to support remote navigation.
  • Development of interactive UI components
  • Focus state system for remote navigation (TV)
  • Motion and transition design for feedback clarity
  • Cross-platform interaction consistency
  • Prototype flows for core user journeys
04
PHASE_04: FINAL TV DESIGN [REMOTE-BASED NAVIGATION]
Designed a streaming experience optimized for large screens and remote-based interaction. Focused on creating clear navigation patterns and strong visual feedback to support directional input.
  • Horizontal browsing system for content discovery
  • Strong focus states to indicate current selection
  • Clear visual feedback for all interactions
  • Optimized layout for readability at distance
  • Navigation designed for directional (D-pad) input
  • Reduced cognitive load through simplified hierarchy
05
PHASE_05: FINAL MOBILE DESIGN [TOUCH INTERACTION]
Adapted the experience for mobile by prioritizing speed, accessibility, and thumb-friendly interaction patterns. Simplified navigation structure to support quick content access.
  • Vertical scrolling for intuitive browsing
  • Thumb-friendly layout and interaction zones
  • Simplified navigation with reduced steps
  • Faster access to key content and actions
  • Touch-based gestures replacing directional input
  • Optimized density for smaller screens
